## Phase 6 — Reflection

### AI giup gi?
- Giup brainstorm nhanh 5 pain point va uoc tinh ton that de co diem bat dau ro rang.
- Goi y cach dien Quick Problem Cards (workflow, bottleneck, metric) de khung bai toan chac hon.
- Phan bien tu goc nhin CFO/Van hanh giup lam ro khi nao Rule-based tot hon.

### AI sai gi / thieu gi?
- So lieu uoc tinh co the qua chung chung, chua co nguon tham chieu noi bo.
- De xuat AI cho bai toan doi soat tai chinh can can nhac ky hon ve tinh audit.

### Minh da sua nhu the nao?
- Dieu chinh metric sang SLA/chi so ro rang hon (T+1, thoi gian xu ly).
- Chuyen huong mot so bai toan ve Rule-based thay vi AI thuan tuy.
- Bo sung buoc HITL va ranh gioi van hanh de giam rui ro.

### Bai hoc rut ra
- AI tot nhat khi dung nhu thought-partner de tao khung, con so lieu cu the can du lieu that.
- Bai toan tai chinh/doi soat can uu tien tinh xac dinh va kha nang giai trinh.
